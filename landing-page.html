Look for immersion,
connections; come with no expectation,
and just enjoy the journey.

Don't chase what everyone before you has pictured.


Our focus is not to bring you to that famous landmark where everyone wants to be taken a picture of nor to bring you to that trendy spot like Mt. Kiltepan of Sagada. We can probably take nice pictures of you, but we're more focused on building lasting moments. Whenever possible, we would like to take a re-route, taking the paths where only few have gone. In there, we hope together we could forge deeper connections and experience meaningful immersions. Hence, we are called Ilaya.
